BỞI NHỮNG TÀI NĂNG

Tại Tập đoàn Filmore, làm việc nhóm có nghĩa là tất cả mọi thứ và bao gồm cả khách hàng của chúng tôi. Với tư cách là một nhóm và đối tác, chúng tôi liên kết chặt chẽ với khách hàng của chúng tôi.

      Bryce Aberg is an industrial capital markets specialist with Cushman & Wakefield, specializing in institutional property leasing and sales, industrial sales, and tenant representation, in all central and south San Diego submarkets, including Miramar, Poway, Kearny Mesa, Sorrento Mesa, SorrentoValley, Rancho Bernardo, Otay Mesa, Chula Vista, and National City, as well as institutional tenant representation in all major West Coast markets.

      Bryce joined Cushman & Wakefield (BRE Commercial) in 2003 and has since completed over 1,500 sale and lease transactions, totaling over 20 million square feet for a total consideration of more than $2.5 billion. Bryce was previously an industrial landlord and tenant specialist with Lee & Associates in Newport Beach, where he was consistently ranked as a top-producing sales associate and was named Rookie of the Year in 2002.

      A graduate of the University of San Diego, Bryce holds a bachelors degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Finance and Marketing. Bryce was a member of Cassidy Turley San Diego’s Leadership Development Group in 2007 and is a committee member for the National Association of Industrial and Office Properties (NAIOP). He is a Society of Industrial and Office Realtors (SIOR) member and a member of the CCIM Institute of Commercial Real Estate. Bryce is also actively involved with the Economic Development Corporation’s Corporate Leaders Advocating Success Skills (CLASS).
